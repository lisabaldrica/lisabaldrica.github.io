About

For over 10 years, I have gained a wide range of graphic design skills. From working with reporters on editorial pages to being part of a creative team on photo shoots. I gained marketing experience working at with both Gottschalks and Valley Children’s. At Gottschalks, I worked with the Visual and Advertising departments to create a cohesive look with the in-store signage and advertising pieces. With Valley Children’s I have worked in the Marketing department creating all types of collateral pieces from large format to small printed pieces. I continue to work with them as a freelancer.
